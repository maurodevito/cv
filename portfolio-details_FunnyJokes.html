Project information

  • Category: Alexa Skill
  • Name: Funny Pranks
  • Project date: 18 December, 2019
  • Project URL: Available Amazon Alexa

Description:

Do you want to make a prank to someone?

Thanks to Funny Pranks, you can plan the reproduction of a noise (for example "ghosts", "barking dog", "earthquake") to scare your friends and not only.

 

The skill is very simple to use, you just need to open it by saying the phrase "Alexa, open Funny Pranks" and then ask "Play a sound".

For any information, simply say: "Help"

 

Discover the wide selection of noises divided into 4 categories:

- scary sounds (for example "ghosts", "screams", etc ...)

- animals (eg "lions", "barking dog", etc ...)

- cities (eg "shots", "earthquake", etc ...)

- people (for example "fart", "burp", etc ...)

... and remember to come back every day to add new noises to your collection

 

Try the "Funny Pranks" skill immediately !!
